我们的意义

对于灾区、农村的贫困地区、城市中的贫困户来说，物资的捐赠，不仅可以在生活和物质上感受社会的温暖和人文关怀，迅速地恢复生产，重建家园，也能使他们的心理上感受到安全和稳定， 这将会比金钱方面的帮助更有意义

Our meaning

For disaster areas, poor rural areas and poor households in cities, material donation can not only feel social warmth and humanistic care in life and material, quickly resume production and rebuild their homes, but also make them feel safe and stable psychologically, This will be more meaningful than money
